Amazon Associates Domain Registration & Quick Checklist

1. Sign into your Amazon Associates account (Associates Central).
2. Open 'Account Settings' -> 'Websites and Mobile Apps'.
3. Click 'Add' and enter your site domain: `laptopvette.com` (no protocol, no trailing slash).
4. Save and wait for Amazon to mark the site as "approved" (you may need to place sample links and receive clicks).

Important steps to complete on your site:
- Keep a visible affiliate disclosure on pages that include affiliate links.
- Ensure each affiliate link uses your Associate tag (we use `tag=laptopvette-20`).
- Add `rel="sponsored nofollow noopener noreferrer"` to affiliate anchors (already applied to CTAs).
- Add a Privacy Policy and Contact page so Amazon can verify ownership.

Verification tips:
- Use SiteStripe to create a test product link and click it from your site to generate activity.
- In Associates Central, check the 'Websites and Mobile Apps' area for status and any email requests.

Amazon policy reminders:
- Never imply endorsements by Amazon.
- Disclose affiliate relationship clearly (done in site content).
- Follow region-specific rules if you operate in multiple countries.
